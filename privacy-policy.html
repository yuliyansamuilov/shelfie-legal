Privacy Policy – Shelfie

Last updated: 04.02.2026

Shelfie ("the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the app.

1. Data Collection

Shelfie does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All data you create in the app (rooms, shelves, boxes, items, labels) is stored locally on your device only.

2. Personal Information

Shelfie does not require account creation and does not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos from your gallery (unless explicitly selected by you)

3. Camera Permission

The app may request camera access only for:

  • Scanning QR codes for box identification

Camera data is processed in real time and is not stored or transmitted.

4. Data Export and Import

If you choose to export or import data:

  • Files are created or read locally on your device
  • Shelfie does not upload or share these files

5. Bluetooth Access

Shelfie may use Bluetooth to connect to a printer for label printing.

No Bluetooth data is collected or stored by the app.

6. Third-Party Services

Shelfie does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Future premium subscriptions may be handled by Google Play billing services, which are subject to Google’s own privacy policies.

7. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, you are responsible for backing up or protecting your data.

Uninstalling the app will permanently delete locally stored data.

8. Children’s Privacy

Shelfie does not target children and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app's functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected in the app store listing.
